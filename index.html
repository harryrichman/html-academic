Gary Fishman

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student at National Taiwan University, advised by Chee-Chun Leung. I am interested in problems in combinatorics, tropical geometry, and number theory.

Before coming to Michigan I studied math at the University of Washington and the Math in Moscow program.
